three.js — Parallax UV

TSL parallax UV mapping on an ice ground plane — layered ambientCG textures with HDR environment lighting. Textures by ambientCG. HDR by HDRI Skies. Based on the Three.js WebGPU parallax UV example.

WebGPU required
