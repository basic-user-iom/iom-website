three.js — VFX linked particles

WebGPU TSL compute particles with nearest-neighbor link ribbons, turbulence, hue rotation, and bloom post-processing. Based on the Three.js WebGPU TSL VFX linked particles example and the original experiment by Christophe Choffel.

WebGPU required

This demo uses WebGPU compute buffers and TSL post-processing. Open it in a browser with WebGPU support (Chrome 113+, Edge 113+, or Firefox Nightly with WebGPU enabled).
